Suggested talking points for a dental clinic:

  • Safety: blocks medication advice; escalates safely.
  • Control: deterministic policy + state machine (no hallucination).
  • Agentic action: creates a callback ticket automatically.
  • Operations: staff sees tasks in the inbox at /staff.
Tip: keep /staff open in a second tab and hit refresh after ticket creation.
